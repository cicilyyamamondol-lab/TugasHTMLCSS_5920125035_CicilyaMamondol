Scrambled Eggs

Eggs re one of my fvourite foods. here is recipe for deliciosly eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a friying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter hs melted dd cream nd eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 second (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
