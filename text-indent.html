Bread History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in france, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep, The breed was used by the french army as senties, messenger and to to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing Briards were used inthe first world war almost to the point of extinection . currently the population of Briards is slowly recovering,Charlemagne ,Napoleon, Thomas,Jefferson and lafayette all owned Briards
